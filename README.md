## &#128075; About Me

Machine Learning engineer with a **B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence &amp; Data Science** and a **Best Paper award**. I work across the applied-ML stack — from **deep learning and computer vision** research to **LLM post-training and reinforcement learning**.

My focus is turning research into systems that actually run: fine-tuning and reinforcement-learning pipelines, evaluation harnesses, and reproducible training under real compute constraints. I care about work that is **measured, reproducible, and honest about its results**.
